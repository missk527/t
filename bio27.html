  7. The host cell can burst.

  9. Three viral diseases.

  29. They prevent the HIV viruses from replicating in the body.
  30. They can often go on to live normal lives.

  40. Viruses infect our cells, use them to replicate, and then burst and destroy them, damaging our bodies.
